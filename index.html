Better health and a happier life

Meditation is a process-oriented undertaking that focuses on the moment, not on the results. So enjoying the moment is key to successful meditation.

6 reasons why you should medidate

Become Happier

Meditation causes the pituitary gland in our brain to secrete endorphins that help elevate mood and have a positive effect on the whole body.

Improves Sleep

Scientific studies have shown that those who meditate enhance their slow wave sleep pattern, which can help fight insomnia.

Better Focus

Meditating allows you to learn how to discipline yourself and this will reflect in your everyday lifestyle.

Reduces stress

Endorphins also act as a means of reducing stress levels in the brain.

Blood Pressure

By decreasing psychological stress and increasing coping ability, blood pressure decreases, helping you pursue a healthier life.

Relieves Pain

Long term practise of meditation can lead to physical changes in the brain that help alter the perception of pain.

Let's get rid of stress!

Stress Meditation

We all face stressful situations throughout our lives, ranging from minor annoyances like traffic jams to more serious worries. Here you can find relaxation techniques that can help you evoke the relaxation response and reduce stress.

Meditation @Work

If some companies have caught on to the great benefits of giving employees a dedicated space and enough time to unplug, most haven't. But there are lots of ways to meditate, and it is definitely possible to extend your practice into your work life.

Sleep Meditation

If you've been struggling to sleep lately, meditating before bed might help. Statistics show that more than 70 million people deal with sleep disorders - in America alone! Insomnia is an unhealthy condition that plagues far too many people.
