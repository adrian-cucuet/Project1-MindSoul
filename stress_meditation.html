Things to know before you start meditation:

  1. Don't expect your brain to go blank.

    One of the biggest misconceptions about meditation is that your mind is supposed to go blank and that you reach a super-Zen state of consciousness. This is typically not true. It's important to keep in mind that you don't have to try to clear thoughts from your brain during meditation.

  2. You do not have to sit cross-legged or hold you hands in any position.

    You can sit in any position that is comfortable to you. Most people sit upright in a chair or on a cushion. Your hands can fall gently in your lap or at your sides.
